LB Protector of the multiverse

LB is teen standing at 5'2 who wears clown makeup. His job as the personal right hand man of the person who is the creator of the KEPO multiverse is to go to every universe and make sure that everything is as it should be.



His day to day life is pretty simple. He wakes up at 9 every morning and gets a strawberry cream chesse bagel and heads to the office at 10. Some days the office is hectic as it is where people are able to go through the universes, so there are always people in the office going from universe to universe. LB is like that right hand man of the Boss. He is almost always in the office and updates the Boss on whatever goes on.
