LB's Powers

LB is the one of 4 people in the entire multiverse who are allowed to jump to any universe at any time they please. Of course there are rules and risks that come with this ability but it is a very special one. LB is granted various special gadgets and weapons being The Boss's right hand man. These gadgets involve memory wiping, brainwashing, invisilbe barrier, and automatic teleporting just to name a few.
