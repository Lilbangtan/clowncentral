LB's Home Universe

Even though LB jumps through various universes everyday, he still has him home universe that he goes back to. Clown Centeral AKA the main universe. Clown Central is where most universe jumping is done. It is also where most information about all of the universes is held. Everyone is Clown Central has on clown makeup almost all the time. At work, at school, even at home, everyone in Clown Central wheres clown makeup. In Clown Central, the style of your clown makeup dictates your status in society.



LB's Living Space

LB is a pretty clean person. His home occasionally gets messy when he is super busy but for the most part he tries to keep it clean. He lives in a decent sized condo in the middle of Clown Central. He lives very close to the ground becuase heights make him nervous. He has his pet peeves about livng in one of the busiest parts of Clown Central but he also thinks that the view from his condo is beautiful and living in such a busy area keeps him from being bored. He has a little black cat named Pancake that he basically worships. She has her own room and he loves to spend way to much money on her.
